Strategic Initiative 1 Activities:Governance

  • Preparation of Policies, Systems and Procedures including legislations on penalties/fines.

Strategic Initiative 2 Activities:Systems and Applications

  • Accreditation/ evaluation of legitimate ICT vendor support ICT research and IT incubation center

Strategic Initiative 3 Activities:Infrastructure and Technology

  • Data Center operation
    • Putting the resources needed such as computer server, connectivity among departments, data storage for sensitive data such as assessment – Treasury and accounting
  • IT clinics/remote diagnostics/HW troubleshooting

Strategic Initiative 4 Activities:Security

  • Inventory of ICT resources/capability
  • Requirements Planning Systems development ISMS administration
  • Prepare ICT organizational plan and develop IT direction for the next 5 years

Strategic Initiative 5 Activities:Information Management

  • Application of ITIL in all workstations and ICT users
  • Deletion of all unauthorized copy of any software’s, cleaning of all hard drives regularly (monthly) and reminding everyone to use only authorized programs

Strategic Initiative 6 Activities:IT Business Continuity

  • Learning and development on ITCP
  • Simulation and testing of ITCP
  • Create an offsite data storage (Use the rebuild the transactions in case of major business interruptions. Provision on how to continue to operate during the disaster)
